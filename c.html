About Us

Housewise is an end to end Property Management Company in India. The company offers rental management and property management services and online rent agreement services. It provides comprehensive services to Non-Resident Indians.


It operates in Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Chennai, Delhi, NCR, Goa, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Thane, Mumbai, and Pune. We are also planning to expand our services in cities like Chandigarh, Goa, etc.

The company was founded in 2015 in Pune by Pryank Agrawal, an IIT graduate. He came up with the idea to solve the problems of the unorganized real estate market. This is how Housewise came into existence with the main aim of providing a Home to the people at their convenience.
